FoxJet follows the TSA guidelines for carry-on baggage. TSA guidelines are as follows:
Carry-on bags shouldn't be larger than 22 inches long, 14 inches wide and 9 inches high including handles and wheels.
Dimensions of personal items should not exceed 18 inches long, 14 inches wide and 8 inches high.
There are no weight restrictions for carry-on luggage.

All FoxJet flights have in-flight entertainment and refreshments. Flights over 2 1/2 hours have a complimentary meal.

All flights within the US and Canada have in-flight WiFi, international flights do not have in-flight WiFi.
